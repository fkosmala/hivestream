How to use ?

Enter the HIVE username who receive the donations into the text input. Don't have HIVE account ?

Click on the button to generate your link for your stream. It's needed to show animation and play sound on the livestream

3Finish

Add a full screen Browser Source and paste the stream link into your stream software and... Share the donation link to boost engagement with tokens !
